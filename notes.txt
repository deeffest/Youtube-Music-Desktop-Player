1.1 Update!
 
- Mini player completely redesigned.
- Fixed application crashes when the window is hidden while closing popup notifications and splash screen.
- Added menu for the tray.
- Now by clicking the exit button, you can hide the window in the tray without interrupting your music listening. (Must be enabled in the settings under the ```Main`` tab).
- Tracks are now converted to ``mp3`` format after downloading.
- Added buttons to control playback in the main window thumbnail and mini player window.
- Added ability to download entire playlists.
- Other changes and improvements.
